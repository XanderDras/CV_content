

Step 1 - Apply: Submit your application online, highlighting your skills and experience.
Step 2 - CV Pre-Screening: Our recruiters review your profile against role requirements.
Step 3 -  Online Assessments (if applicable): Some roles may include technical tests or the Nokia Talent Discovery assessment prior to the interview.
Step 4 -  Interview: A 60–90-minute interview, conducted online or onsite, depending on role and location. For some positions, a second-step interview may be organised to dive deeper into your skills. The interview is a chance for you to get to know us - our culture, ways of working, and opportunities - just as much as it is for us to learn about you.
Step 5 -  Decision & Offer: After careful evaluation, we provide feedback and make hiring decisions promptly. Successful candidates receive an offer and guidance on the next steps
