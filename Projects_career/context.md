# Project Selection Guidelines

## How to choose projects for a CV
When adapting the CV for a specific company, use the following filtering criteria:

1.  **Relevance over Recency:** Prioritize projects that use the exact tech stack or solve the same business problems mentioned in the job description, even if they aren't your most recent.
2.  **Scale and Impact:** If applying for a senior role, prioritize projects that show architectural design, leadership, or massive scale. If applying for a junior/mid role, emphasize hands-on coding, problem-solving, and clean execution.
3.  **Domain Match:** Consider the context of the role i am applying to, choosing correctly the projects for that position

## Adaptation Rules
*   Never include more than 4-5 projects on a single CV to save space for work experience.
*   Always tweak the bullet points to emphasize the skills the employer is asking for (e.g., if the employer wants AWS experience, make sure the AWS aspect of your project is listed first).
